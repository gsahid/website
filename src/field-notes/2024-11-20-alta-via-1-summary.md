## Getting there and back
- I flew from Berlin to Venice and arrived at midnight; I decided to book one of the sleeping pods at the airport because I knew I'd need to take the bus to Cortina d'Ampezzo in the next morning, but I highly wouldn't recommend it. There are probably other better ways to reach Venice from Berlin especially if you're not really constrained by time, so I highly recommend looking for alternatives or spend a day in Venice if possible.
- There is no direct bus to Lago di Braies, so what I did was to take the bus to Cortina d'Ampezzo, and take another bus in the direction of Toblach and stopped in Lago di Braies.
